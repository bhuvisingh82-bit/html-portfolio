Top 5 Video-Games Ever According To Me

My Top 5 Video Games Of All Time :

1.RED DEAD REDEMPTION

*Red Dead Redemption 2* stands above all other games because it isn’t just a game—it’s an experience. Its world feels alive with every detail, from wildlife behavior to NPC interactions, while the storytelling delivers raw emotion and depth rarely seen in gaming. It combines freedom, realism, and narrative impact in a way no other title has matched.

2.GOD OF WAR

*God of War* is an absolute masterpiece—cinematic storytelling, brutal yet fluid combat, and a heartfelt father-son journey wrapped in Greek/Norse mythology. Easily one of the greatest action-adventure games ever made.

3.Black Myth Wukong

*Black Myth: Wukong* is stunning—jaw-dropping visuals, fluid combat, and a fresh take on Chinese mythology that feels both epic and groundbreaking.

4.UNCHARTED

*Uncharted* is an exhilarating adventure that blends cinematic storytelling, thrilling exploration, and heart-pounding action. With its charismatic characters and breathtaking set pieces, it feels like playing through a blockbuster movie.

5.DETROIT BECOME HUMAN

*Detroit: Become Human* is an exceptional narrative-driven game that puts your choices at the heart of the story. With stunning visuals, deep moral dilemmas, and branching paths, it delivers an emotional and immersive experience that feels truly alive.
